# Theorem

Consider the domain $\mathbb T_L = (\mathbb R / L\mathbb Z)^n$. For $f \in H^1(\mathbb T_L)$ satisfying
\[
  \int_{\mathbb T_L} f(x) \, dx = 0,
\]
there exists $C > 0$ such that
\[
    \|f\|_{L^2} \leq C \|\nabla f\|_{L^2}.
\]
